Featured Projects

Convergence

Convergence is an experimental gameplay systems project developed in Unity that explores adaptive enemy AI behaviour in wave-based survival games.

The project introduces a dynamic enemy merging system where nearby enemies combine to form stronger variants, creating emergent gameplay behaviour and forcing players to adapt their combat strategies.

Brawl Shooters

Brawl Shooters is a top-down wave-based action shooter built in Unity. The project focuses on building scalable gameplay systems including enemy AI behaviour, wave spawning mechanics, and player progression.

Players fight increasingly difficult enemy waves, collect experience, and upgrade their abilities while surviving inside a confined arena.
