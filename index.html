Convergence

Convergence is an experimental gameplay systems project developed in Unity that explores adaptive AI behaviour in wave-based survival games.

The project introduces a dynamic enemy merging system where nearby enemies combine into stronger variants, creating emergent gameplay behaviour and forcing players to adapt their combat strategies.

Brawl Shooters is a top-down roguelike shooter focused on building scalable gameplay systems such as enemy AI behaviour, wave spawning, player progression, and combat mechanics.
