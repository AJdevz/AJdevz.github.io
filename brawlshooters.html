Brawl Shooters

Brawl Shooters is a top-down wave survival shooter developed in Unity that serves as the foundational project for exploring scalable gameplay mechanics and enemy AI systems. The project focuses on implementing robust player combat systems, enemy behaviour, and progression mechanics that can evolve dynamically during gameplay.

Project Overview

Brawl Shooters provides a solid gameplay foundation built on core mechanics including player movement, weapon systems, and enemy spawning. The project explores how these fundamental systems can scale efficiently as gameplay complexity increases.

The game emphasizes the player's ability to survive increasingly difficult waves of enemies while managing resources through an upgrade system and progression mechanics.

Core Features

  • Player movement and shooting mechanics
  • Health and damage systems with visual feedback
  • Wave-based enemy spawning system
  • Enemy AI chase behaviour and collision detection
  • Experience and leveling progression
  • Weapon upgrade systems
  • Boss spawn mechanics for variety
  • HUD systems (health, experience, wave counter)
  • Performance optimisation for multiple AI agents

Gameplay Mechanics

Player Systems

  • Top-down perspective with responsive movement controls
  • Weapon system with firing mechanics and cooldown management
  • Health management with visual damage feedback

Enemy AI

  • Enemy proximity detection for awareness
  • Chase behaviour toward the player
  • Scalable difficulty through enemy numbers and health scaling
  • Wave progression with increasing complexity

Progression Systems

  • Experience collection from defeated enemies
  • Level-up system unlocking weapon upgrades
  • Weapon upgrade management between waves

Technical Implementation

The project demonstrates solid understanding of gameplay programming fundamentals including:

  • Object pooling for efficient enemy spawning
  • State management for player and enemy states
  • Event-driven systems for damage and progression
  • Scriptable objects for data configuration
  • Performance monitoring with large numbers of active enemies

AI Performance Optimisation

Managing large numbers of AI agents simultaneously is a key challenge in survival games. Brawl Shooters implements several optimisation strategies:

  • Object pooling to reduce instantiation overhead
  • Efficient pathfinding and behaviour trees
  • LOD (Level of Detail) systems for distant enemies
  • Wave timing to prevent performance spikes

Learning Outcomes

This project served as the experimental bed for gameplay systems that informed the development of Convergence, providing valuable insights into:

  • Scalable enemy spawning and wave design
  • Player feedback and combat responsiveness
  • AI behaviour implementation and iteration
  • Performance considerations at scale
