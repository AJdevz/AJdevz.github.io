Gameplay Overview

Brawl Shooters is a fast-paced top-down roguelike shooter where the player must survive increasing waves of zombies. Enemies continuously spawn around the arena and become more difficult as the game progresses.

Every 5 waves a boss zombie appears with significantly increased health. Players collect XP orbs dropped by enemies which allow them to level up and upgrade their weapons.

Core Gameplay Systems

  • Wave spawning system
  • Enemy AI chase behaviour
  • XP and player leveling system
  • Weapon upgrade mechanics
  • Boss enemy every 5 waves

Controls

  • Move – WASD
  • Aim – Mouse
  • Shoot – Left Mouse Button
  • Pause – ESC

Technical Details

  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Platform: PC
  • Genre: Top-down roguelike shooter

Enemy Types

Basic Zombie – slow movement with low health.

Boss Zombie – large enemy with significantly higher health that appears every 5 waves.

System Requirements

Recommended
