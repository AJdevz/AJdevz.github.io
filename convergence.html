Convergence

Convergence is a gameplay systems prototype built as an evolution of Brawl Shooters. The project experiments with adaptive enemy behaviours and introduces a dynamic enemy merging mechanic where enemies combine to form stronger variants.

Game Modes

Classic Mode

A traditional wave-based survival mode where the player fights increasing enemy waves similar to Brawl Shooters.

Merge Mode

Enemies dynamically merge when close to each other, forming stronger enemy types with increased stats. This forces players to prioritise targets and manage enemy positioning.

Technical Systems

  • Enemy proximity detection
  • Dynamic enemy stat scaling
  • Enemy merging behaviour
  • Wave spawning and difficulty scaling
  • Gameplay debugging and optimisation
