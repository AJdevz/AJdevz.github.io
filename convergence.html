Convergence

Convergence is an experimental gameplay systems project that expands on the core mechanics of Brawl Shooters. The project introduces new enemy behaviours including a dynamic merging mechanic where enemies combine to form stronger variants.

Game Modes

Classic Mode

Traditional wave-based gameplay inspired by Brawl Shooters where players fight increasing numbers of enemies.

Merge Mode

Enemies dynamically merge when close to each other, forming stronger enemy types. This forces the player to adapt their strategy and prioritise targets.

Technical Systems

  • Enemy proximity detection
  • Dynamic enemy stat scaling
  • Enemy merging behaviour
  • Wave spawning and difficulty scaling
  • Gameplay debugging and optimisation
